FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know about banking with Zitch. Still stuck? Email support@zitch.ng.

Is Zitch a licensed and safe place to keep my money?

Zitch operates in partnership with CBN-licensed financial institutions, and customer funds are held with regulated partners. Every transaction is protected by your secret PIN, bank-grade BVN/NIN and face verification, and an append-only audit trail records every movement.

How do I open a Zitch account?

Download the Zitch app from the App Store or Google Play, sign up with your phone number, verify the OTP, set a password and a 4-digit PIN, and you're ready. Opening an account takes a few minutes.

What can I do on Zitch?

Send money to any Nigerian bank, buy airtime and data on all networks, pay electricity and cable bills, buy exam pins, fund betting wallets, get a virtual dollar card, grow your savings, and hold and convert NGN, USD, GBP, CAD and CNY.

How does WhatsApp banking work, and is it secure?

Link your phone number once, then bank by chatting with Zitch on WhatsApp in plain language — for example, “send 5k to my brother John.” Zitch can draft the transaction, but no money ever moves until you approve it with your fingerprint, Face ID or PIN.

How fast are transfers and bill payments?

Zitch-to-Zitch transfers are instant, transfers to other Nigerian banks arrive in seconds, and airtime, data and electricity tokens are usually delivered within a few seconds.

How do I get help or contact Zitch support?

You can reach the Zitch team on WhatsApp, by email at support@zitch.ng, or by phone at 08166938327, 8am to 8pm daily. You can also follow @zitchng on Instagram and Facebook for updates.
